Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

At AcmeTech Careers, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our website, apply for a job, or communicate with us. By accessing this site, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better recruitment experience for our users. The types of data we may collect include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and resume details submitted through the application form.
  • Technical Information: Browser type, IP address, device information, and pages visited.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to remember user preferences and improve navigation experience.
  • Communication Data: Messages or inquiries you send through our contact form.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process and evaluate your job application.
  • To contact you regarding interviews, updates, or relevant opportunities.
  • To improve website performance and functionality.
  • To ensure compliance with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and only share information when necessary. Your data may be shared with:

  • Authorized HR departments and hiring managers within AcmeTech.
  • Third-party service providers assisting in site maintenance or analytics, under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal authorities, if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of AcmeTech and users.

4. Data Security

We employ appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes secure servers, encrypted communication, and restricted data access.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. Once the retention period ends, your information will be securely deleted or anonymized.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access and receive a copy of your data.
  • Right to request correction or deletion of inaccurate information.
  • Right to withdraw consent for processing (where applicable).
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

7. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us track usage patterns and preferences. You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features may not function properly if cookies are turned off.

8. External Links

Our site may contain links to external websites or job application portals. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage users to review their respective privacy policies.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect legal or operational changes. Any modifications will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
